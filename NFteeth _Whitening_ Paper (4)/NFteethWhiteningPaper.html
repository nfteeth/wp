Betoothed is a fast-paced, Bejeweled-style puzzle game designed for PC, Mac, and mobile devices that offers an engaging way to mine the NFTteeth token while having fun. Players will match and clear themed tiles to earn rewards, compete in leaderboards, and participate in exclusive events.

Core Features:

        •        Token Mining Through Gameplay – Players earn NFTteeth tokens by matching and clearing tiles in skill-based gameplay.

        •        NFT Integration – Levelable Teeth Boost Rewards – Players can use their NFTteeth collection to enhance their gameplay. Leveling up Tooth NFTs increases their mining efficiency, allowing for greater rewards and special in-game bonuses.

        •        Tournaments & Leaderboards – Compete in scheduled events for exclusive prizes and ranking rewards.

        •        Burn Events – Special challenges where players can sacrifice tokens for rare in-game rewards and benefits.

        •        Cross-Platform Play – Seamlessly switch between PC, Mac, and mobile to keep progress intact.
